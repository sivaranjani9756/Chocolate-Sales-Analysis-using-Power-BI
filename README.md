# Chocolate-Sales-Analysis
## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing chocolate sales data to evaluate overall business performance. 
The goal is to identify sales trends, top-selling products, and revenue distribution across regions using interactive visualizations.


## Business Objectives
- Analyze total sales and revenue trends
- Identify top-performing chocolate products
- Compare sales performance across regions
- Support data-driven business decisions


## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Data Modeling
- DAX (Measures & KPIs)
- Data Visualization

## Dashboard Features
- KPI cards for total sales and revenue
- Product-wise and category-wise sales analysis
- Region-wise sales comparison
- Interactive slicers for dynamic filtering


## Key Insights
- Identified best-selling chocolate products
- Observed sales trends over time
- Highlighted high and low performing regions
